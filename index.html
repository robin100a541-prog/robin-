Who I Am

A creator
of life.

I'm Robin Aoun — 19 years old, from Skövde, Sweden. I don't fit into one box. I'm a footballer, a creator, an entrepreneur, and someone who genuinely believes that with the right system and the right mindset, anything becomes possible.

My mission is to build a disciplined life around three things: inspiration, development, and high performance. Not just in football. Not just in business. In everything. The way I train. The way I create. The way I think. Every single area of my life is something I'm actively building and improving.

I've played football across multiple countries — including Portugal with Sporting CP — created content that's reached hundreds of thousands of people, and built businesses from scratch at 19. I speak Swedish, English, Arabic, and French. I communicate through action.

What drives me isn't the destination — it's the discipline it takes to get there, and the person you become along the way.

Want to know more about me or what I do? Reach out — I respond personally.
